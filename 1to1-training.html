Focused Training for Core Skills

Our 1-to-1 sessions are designed to help you and your dog nail the essential skills that make everyday life easier. Whether you're building on what you learned in our group classes or starting fresh, we'll work together at your dog's pace.

Ideal follow-on from Puppy Pre-School or Canine College! If your dog needs a bit more practice on a specific skill, our private sessions are the perfect way to top up their training.

Every session is tailored to your goals. We focus on positive reinforcement methods that build your dog's confidence and strengthen your bond.

Skills We Can Help You Master

Our private sessions focus on building reliable foundations with the everyday skills that matter most.

Loose-Lead Walking

Enjoy relaxed walks without the pulling. We'll teach your dog to walk calmly by your side.

Reliable Recall

Build a strong "come when called" response so you can give your dog more freedom safely.

Sit, Stay & Down

Master these essential commands that form the building blocks of good manners.

Polite Greetings

Stop jumping up and teach calm, controlled behaviour when meeting people.

Settle & Calmness

Help your dog learn to relax and settle, both at home and out and about.

Wait & Impulse Control

Teach patience at doorways, mealtimes, and when distractions appear.

Questions & Answers

Our 1-to-1 sessions are perfect for dogs who need focused practice on basic skills. They're especially great as a follow-on from our Puppy Pre-School or Canine College classes if your dog needs extra help with specific commands like recall, lead walking, or settling. They're also ideal for dogs who find group environments too distracting.

Our private sessions focus on foundation skills and basic obedience training. For dogs with complex behavioural challenges such as severe reactivity, aggression, or deep-rooted anxiety, we recommend consulting a qualified clinical animal behaviourist who specialises in these areas.
