About Us

Part of the RSPCA Cornwall Family

Proper Dog Cornwall is the commercial dog services brand of RSPCA Cornwall Branch. We offer professional dog training and grooming services, with all proceeds supporting the charity's vital animal welfare work.

Our team combines professional expertise with a genuine passion for animal welfare. When you choose Proper Dog Cornwall, you're not just getting quality services for your dog - you're helping rescue animals across Cornwall.
