Why Train With Proper Dog Cornwall?

Our team works hands-on every day with rescue dogs facing complex behavioural challenges. This gives us a unique depth of understanding that typical trainers simply don't have.

  • Experienced with all behaviours From nervous rescues to bouncy adolescents
  • Force-free methods only Building confidence, not fear
  • Secure outdoor paddocks Safe space to learn and practise
