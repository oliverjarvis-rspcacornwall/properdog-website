Why Train With Proper Dog Cornwall?

Our team works hands-on every day with rescue dogs of all ages and temperaments. This gives us unique expertise in positive, force-free training methods that really work.

  • Experienced with shy puppies We go at your puppy's pace, never pushing
  • Fun, game-based learning Puppies learn best when they're having fun
  • Secure outdoor paddocks Safe space for off-lead socialisation

Training With Purpose

Every penny funds our rescue work - helping vulnerable animals across Cornwall find loving homes.
