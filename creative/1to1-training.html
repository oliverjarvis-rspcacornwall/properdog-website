Questions & Answers

Our 1-to-1 sessions are perfect for dogs who need focused practice on basic skills. They're especially great as a follow-on from our group classes if your dog needs extra help with specific commands like recall, lead walking, or settling. Also ideal for dogs who find group environments too distracting.

Our private sessions focus on foundation skills and basic obedience training. For dogs with complex behavioural challenges such as severe reactivity, aggression, or deep-rooted anxiety, we recommend consulting a qualified clinical animal behaviourist who specialises in these areas.

Sessions are held at our Centre in St Columb (TR9 6JS). We can also come to you for home visits across Cornwall, with a travel charge of ¬£1 per mile from our Centre.
