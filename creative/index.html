Why Choose Us

Why Train With Proper Dog Cornwall?

Our trainers work hands-on, every single day, with rescue dogs facing a wide range of challenges. This gives us a unique depth of understanding and proven positive techniques.

  • 100% Positive Methods

    Force-free, reward-based training that builds confidence and is backed by science.

  • Experienced Trainers

    Years of real-world experience working with dogs of all backgrounds and temperaments.

  • Safe, Secure Environment

    Classes held in enclosed paddocks at our Rehoming Centre in St Columb.

Making a Difference

Training With a Purpose

When you train with us, you're doing more than helping your own dog. Every pound from our classes directly funds our rescue and rehoming work, providing food, shelter, and care for Cornwall's most vulnerable animals.
